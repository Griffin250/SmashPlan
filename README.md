# 📱 SmashPlan – Work Planning Made Easy at Smashed

SmashPlan is the ultimate scheduling app tailored for the **Smashed** restaurant team. It simplifies shift planning, communication, and task management — empowering managers to organize work efficiently and enabling employees to clearly see and manage their responsibilities.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 👔 For Managers (Boss Mode)
- **Create & Publish Schedules:** Plan daily, weekly, or monthly shifts with an intuitive drag-and-drop UI.
- **Assign Staff Roles:** Define roles (e.g., kitchen, cashier, server) and allocate them per shift.
- **Instant Notifications:** Alert staff about new shifts, changes, or urgent updates.
- **Track Availability:** Review staff availability and time-off requests to avoid conflicts.

### 👷‍♀️ For Staff (Worker Mode)
- **View Schedule Anytime:** Log in and see your work schedule from any device.
- **Shift Swap Requests:** Easily request shift changes or swaps with teammates.
- **Reminders:** Get notifications for upcoming shifts, tasks, or important events.

---

## 💡 Why SmashPlan?
- Built specifically for **Smashed**, with a focus on your unique workflow.
- Reduces miscommunication and last-minute confusion.
- Empowers the team with clarity and autonomy over their schedules.
- Modern, mobile-first design that’s simple and intuitive for all users.
